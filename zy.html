In folk culture,
there are many taboos and worship that are considered by the new generation to be a form of feudal superstition, dregs and foolishness.

But the younger generation also has its own contemporary superstitions, like repost koi. Both are in fact the spiritual quest and hope of the people for a better future.

The Wheel of Destiny, uses a new generation of forms to update, adapt and popularise old culture, preserving the interesting folklore passed down from mouth to mouth.
