Dannie's Website

Here is Dannie's personal website, which contains my personal work for 2020-2024. Dannie is an designer from Fuzhou, Fujian.

I graduated from the Shanghai Academy of Visual Arts in 2024, specialising in design and curation, and is also an art critic. I currently live and work in Shanghai and my artistic practice is very broad, with concerns ranging from folk culture to Queer culture to imagining the future. Through playful ways of building ironic narratives, deconstructing visual symbols and recreating their creation, I explore larger issues such as consumerism, technology, tradition and politics.
