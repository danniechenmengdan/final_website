Bank of Eternal life In today's society some people will choose jobs that come at the cost of their health in order to gain money quickly. Those at the top of the pyramid also use money to get a healthy and even longer life. The length of life is thus directly linked to money

If the status quo is taken to extremes, this indirect conversion is turned into a direct conversion. When life banks emerge, time and life can be converted directly into money. Life expectancy would then quickly replace the existence of gold as the general equivalent. The extreme stratification of social classes, where life expectancy is directly equated with money, or even where life is directly equated with money. Would the poor and the rich still belong to the same species?

The Bank of Life is a bank that exchanges the length of an individual's life for money. It is a living building made up of organic lighthouse jellyfish and inorganic matter.
